Emulating Early Interfaces: Windows 95

  • Windos 95 Online Emulator
    • (Follow along) Try out themes in the Control Panel: Start > Settings > Control Panel, then select the "Desktop Themes" tile
    • (10 min) Poke around the Control Panel, what's familiar, what's missing that you would expect today? We'll go around the room sharing our observations.
    • (30 minutes) Exercise - Paint
      • (Follow along) Open Paint: Start > Programs > Accessories > Paint
      • Explore the interface, tools, and features. Try drawing something What do you notice? How is it different from MacPaint from last week? Modern drawing?
      • At the end of 30 minutes we'll share our drawings and observations.
    • (30 minutes) Exercise - Browse example.com, danluu.com, info.cern.ch (the first website), and then try looking up older websites. What breaks, what works?

Homework
